01Industrial Motivation

Manual Inspection Limitations

Current road inspection is slow, subjective, and unsafe for field workers operating in live traffic conditions.

High Economic Impact

Poor road conditions increase accident risk and result in billions in vehicle repair and maintenance costs annually.

Cost Barrier of Advanced Systems

LiDAR-based and deep learning systems require expensive hardware, limiting large-scale municipal deployment.

02Pipeline Overview & Architecture

1. Image Acquisition: RGB camera mounted on vehicle captures continuous road surface frames.
2. Preprocessing: Grayscale conversion, noise filtering and illumination normalization improve detection robustness.
3. Geometric Correction (Homography): Perspective view is transformed into a bird’s-eye view for accurate defect measurement.
4. Texture-Based Feature Extraction: Local Binary Patterns highlight surface irregularities while suppressing shadows and markings.
5. Segmentation & Contour Detection: Morphological operations localize pothole boundaries and compute defect area.
6. Severity Grading: Damage ratio is calculated and defects are classified into repair priority levels.
